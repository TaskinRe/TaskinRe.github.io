Who is Stickman?

In the heart of ancient caves, where shadows danced and firelight flickered, our ancestors painted tales with crude strokes of pigment. Amongst the menagerie of beasts and scenes, the stickman emerged as a simple yet enduring figure. Millennia passed, civilizations rose and fell, yet the stickman endured. From ancient scrolls to modern screens, it has evolved, a symbol of human expression. Today, in the digital age, the stickman thrives, uniting us in laughter and storytelling, a testament to the timeless power of simplicity in our shared narrative.
